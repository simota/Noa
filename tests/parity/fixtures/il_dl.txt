## input:
AA\r\nBB\r\nCC\e[2;1H\e[L\e[1;1H\e[M
## expect:

BB
CC

# cursor: 0,0
## why:
IL at row 2 pushes BB/CC down one row, inserting a blank line at the cursor
(the bottom row falls off). DL at row 1 then deletes the top row (AA is
already gone — the inserted blank moved up), shifting everything up and
back-filling a blank at the bottom of the scroll region.
