## mode: attrs
## input:
\e[31mAB\e7\e[m\e[3;1HCD\e8EF
## expect:
0: [0-3] "ABEF" fg=1
2: [0-1] "CD"
# cursor: 0,4
## why:
DECSC/DECRC: ESC 7 saves position AND pen (SGR) state; ESC 8 restores both.
After the save the pen is reset and CD prints unstyled elsewhere, then DECRC
must bring back column 2 of row 0 and the red foreground, so EF continues the
original run seamlessly (one style run "ABEF").
