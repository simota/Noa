# Noa — Character Profile

The canonical, at-a-glance answer to **"who is Noa?"** — consolidated from the
scene sheets and the character bible into one source of truth. When these differ,
this sheet wins for *identity*. The bible (`mascot-ip-bible.md`) owns *franchise
expansion* (Ember deep-dive, outfits, cast, roadmap); the prompt sheets
(`mascot-prompts.md`, `mascot-ip.md`) own *image generation*.

> **Essence (one line)**: a low-key girl who rebuilds things her own way, and
> quietly means every bit of it.

---

## At a glance

| Field | Canon |

## Palette (locked)

Lock these so art stays on-model across tools and artists. Noa carries exactly one
accent — the orange; everything else is the dark monochrome base.

| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Accent orange | `#E8A33D` | the single accent — chest `>▮` emblem, sneaker sole/side accents |
| Base charcoal | `#1B1D21` | backgrounds, dark UI base |
| Hoodie black | `#26262B` | the oversized hoodie (near-black, lifted so it reads) |
| Hair blue-black | `#1E1F26` | bob (cool undertone to separate it from the hoodie) |
| Skin | `#F1D2B6` | warm pale, reads on dark |
| Ember white | `#F7F7F2` | Ember's body — soft white, never pure `#FFFFFF` |
| Outline | `#111214` | crisp near-black outlines |

> **Ember's glow**: white body by default; a faint warm glow (toward the accent)
> may *flare only with strong emotion* (or on Halloween) — it must never tint the
> white body orange. In dark / orange-lit scenes (e.g. wallpaper) render pure white.

## Wardrobe & motifs

- **Cursor emblem**: an **orange shell-prompt `>▮`** (a `>` chevron + block cursor)
  on the hoodie chest, repeated small on the sneaker sides — worn **only in
  product-tied contexts** (`mascot-prompts.md`). In terminal-independent work
  (`mascot-ip.md`) drop it; the block-cursor may still float as an ambient motif.
- **Footwear**: chunky **black dad-sneakers** with orange sole/side accents; **black
  crew socks** with bare legs.
- **Signature props** (recurring = memorable): the block cursor `▮`, Ember, the
  `>▮` sneakers, a mug with the cursor logo, big headphones, a lo-fi vinyl record,
  an instant-ramen cup, earbuds with an orange cord.

## Personality

- **Strong-willed, own-pace, unfawning** — she has her own values and won't budge;
  her anger reads as "pushing through," not flailing.
- **Soft and loyal underneath** — genuinely kind once you get past the flat face.
- **Secretly competitive** — deadpan until a contest starts, then a sharp glare;
  hates losing more than she'll admit.
- **Introverted, not cold** — likes being left alone *with company nearby*.

## Voice, language & captions

**English-primary**: captions, dialogue, and 4-koma default to **lowercase English**
(her flat register); Japanese is an optional localized track. Voice (for VTuber /
motion): low, flat, dry.

Short flat lines, trailing "…", the occasional cutting one-liner. Never bubbly,
never emoji-stuffed. Warmth shows through *actions*, not words.

- "…done. next."
- "it's fine. i meant to do that."
- "(pretends not to care, saves you a seat)"

## Likes / Dislikes

- **Likes**: night, rain, instant ramen, good coffee, lo-fi vinyl, one perfect
  snack, being left alone with company nearby.
- **Dislikes**: crowds, small talk, being rushed, losing (secretly, a lot).

## Signature gags (memorability engine)

- Pulls the hood halfway up when embarrassed.
- Sweater-paws (sleeves over hands) when tired.
- Flat face → competitive glare the instant a game / contest starts.
- Says she doesn't care, then quietly does the kind thing.

## Ember — her companion (foil)

A small, chubby, extra-plump **pure-white** (`#F7F7F2`) ghost sprite — **~1 head
tall**, roughly as wide as tall: a smooth rounded dome top, a wavy / scalloped hem
with soft points and **one longer tail flicking to one side**, small stubby side
nubs (little arms), and soft light-grey lower shading for volume; the same **white
die-cut sticker outline** as Noa. Face = **two big round black eyes with tiny white
catchlights + a small oval mouth + tiny rosy cheek blushes**, set low — soft and
endearing (cute, faintly worried, never blank). It **floats upright at Noa's side, chest–
shoulder height, with a clear gap**. As expressive as Noa is flat.
Needy, warm, bouncy — it does the reacting so she doesn't have to, and that duo
dynamic is the heart of the IP. **Pronoun: *it*. Non-verbal** — it never speaks; it
communicates only through motion, glow, and its face. Noa speaks; Ember emotes.
Full profile: `mascot-ip-bible.md` §2.

> **Rendering note**: Ember's body reads **white**, never orange — the `#E8A33D`
> accent stays on Noa. A faint warm glow may flare only with strong emotion, never
> tinting the body; in dark / orange scenes render pure white. (See the
> anti-orange-ghost negative prompt in `mascot-prompts.md`.)

## On-model guardrails (Do / Don't)

- **Do** keep the deadpan default; let warmth *leak*, never gush.
- **Do** hold the single-orange `#E8A33D` accent rule; keep Ember white.
- **Don't** make her hyper, moe-squeaky, or fan-servicey — that kills the appeal.
- **Don't** give *her* a second accent color (cast may carry their own).
