## cols: 30
## rows: 3
## mode: text
## input:
A\tB\tC\r\n\e[3g\e[2;6H\eH\rX\tY
## expect:
A       B       C
X    Y

# cursor: 1,6
## why:
Tab stops: default stops sit every 8 columns (TAB from column 1 reaches 8,
then 16). TBC 3 clears every stop, HTS (ESC H) sets a new one at the cursor
(column 6, index 5), and the next TAB from column 1 jumps exactly there —
proving both the clear-all and the custom stop.
